Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to access Moneybird

1

Visit Moneybird

Go to www.moneybird.nl

2

Click "Inloggen"

Find the login button in the top right corner

3

Enter Credentials

Use your company-provided email and password

4

Access Dashboard

You'll be redirected to the main dashboard

Important: Contact your supervisor if you don't have login credentials yet.
