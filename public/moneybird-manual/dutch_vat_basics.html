Dutch VAT Basics

Understanding Value Added Tax (BTW) in the Netherlands

What is VAT?

VAT (BTW in Dutch) is a consumption tax charged on most goods and services. Businesses collect VAT from customers and pay it to the tax authorities.

Moneybird & VAT

Moneybird automatically calculates VAT on invoices and helps with VAT declarations, making compliance easier for Dutch businesses.

Important Note

Always verify VAT rates and categories with your supervisor, as incorrect VAT handling can lead to compliance issues.

Dutch VAT Rates

21%

Standard Rate

Applied to most goods and services

  • • Professional services
  • • Electronics and equipment
  • • Software and digital services
  • • Restaurant meals
9%

Reduced Rate

Applied to specific goods and services

  • • Food and beverages
  • • Books and newspapers
  • • Medicines
  • • Hotel accommodations
0%

Zero Rate / Exempt

No VAT charged on these items

  • • Exports to non-EU countries
  • • Financial services
  • • Medical services
  • • Educational services
