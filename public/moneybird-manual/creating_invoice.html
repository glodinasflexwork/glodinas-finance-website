Creating Your First Invoice

Follow these steps to create a professional invoice

1

Navigate to Facturen

Click on "Facturen" in the main menu

2

Click "Nieuwe factuur"

Start creating a new invoice

3

Select Client

Choose from existing contacts or add new

4

Add Invoice Details

Date, reference, and payment terms

5

Add Line Items

Description, quantity, and price

6

Review & Send

Check details and send to client

Tip: Always double-check VAT rates and client details before sending!
