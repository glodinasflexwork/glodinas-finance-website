Basic Accounting Terms

Essential accounting concepts every Moneybird user should understand

Why Learn These Terms?

Understanding basic accounting terminology will help you navigate Moneybird more effectively and communicate better with your colleagues about financial matters.

Foundation for Success

These terms form the foundation of all accounting work and will be essential throughout your career at Glodinas Finance B.V.

1

Accounts Payable (AP)

Money your company owes to suppliers for goods/services purchased on credit

2

Accounts Receivable (AR)

Money owed to your company by customers for goods/services sold on credit

3

Revenue

Total income generated from sales before expenses are deducted

4

Expenses

Costs incurred in the process of generating revenue

5

Profit & Loss (P&L)

Statement showing revenues, costs, and expenses over a period

6

Balance Sheet

Snapshot of company's assets, liabilities, and equity at a specific time

7

VAT (Value Added Tax)

Consumption tax added at each stage of the supply chain

8

Journal Entry

First step in accounting where financial transactions are recorded

9

Reconciliation

Comparing records to ensure figures are accurate and in agreement

10

Trial Balance

Worksheet showing all ledger balances to check arithmetical equality
