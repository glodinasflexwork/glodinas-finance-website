Document Types in Moneybird

Moneybird handles various types of financial documents. Understanding each type helps you categorize and process them correctly.

Why This Matters

Proper document handling ensures accurate financial records, compliance with regulations, and smooth business operations.

Sales Invoice (Factuur)

Document sent to customers requesting payment for goods or services

  • • Contains customer details and itemized charges
  • • Includes VAT calculations and payment terms
  • • Creates accounts receivable entry

Purchase Invoice (Inkoopfactuur)

Bill received from suppliers for goods or services purchased

  • • Records business expenses
  • • Contains supplier information and costs
  • • Creates accounts payable entry

Quote (Offerte)

Proposal sent to potential customers with pricing information

  • • Non-binding price estimate
  • • Can be converted to invoice when accepted
  • • Helps track potential sales

Credit Note (Creditnota)

Document issued to reduce the amount owed by a customer

  • • Used for returns, discounts, or corrections
  • • Reduces accounts receivable
  • • Must reference original invoice
