Advanced Reporting & Analysis

Unlocking deeper insights from your Moneybird data

Beyond Basic Reports

While Moneybird offers standard reports, its true power lies in its ability to generate custom reports and allow for detailed data analysis. This helps in making informed business decisions.

Why Go Deeper?

In-depth analysis helps identify trends, forecast future performance, optimize cash flow, and ensure compliance with financial regulations. It moves beyond just recording transactions to understanding their impact.

Key Features for In-depth Analysis

Customizable Reports

Tailor reports to your specific needs by selecting data points, date ranges, and filters. Create reports for specific projects, clients, or expense categories.

Advanced Filtering & Grouping

Utilize powerful filtering options to drill down into specific data sets. Group transactions by various criteria for a more granular view of your finances.

Export for External Analysis

Export data in various formats (e.g., CSV, Excel) for further analysis in external tools like spreadsheets or business intelligence software.

Integration with Other Tools

Moneybird can integrate with other financial tools and platforms, allowing for a more holistic view of financial data and automated workflows.
