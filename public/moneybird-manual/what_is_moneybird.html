What is Moneybird?

Moneybird is a comprehensive online accounting software designed specifically for entrepreneurs, freelancers, and small businesses in the Netherlands.

Why Glodinas Finance Uses Moneybird

It streamlines our financial processes, ensures compliance with Dutch tax regulations, and provides real-time insights into our business performance.
